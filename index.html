Meet Maya

Hi, I’m Maya! I’ve grown up surrounded by dogs and have always felt a special connection with them. Combining my love for animals with my passion for staying active and outdoors is a dream come true. Whether it’s a brisk jog in the park or a leisurely stroll through the neighborhood, I treat every walk as an adventure. I understand that every dog has their own unique personality and energy level, and I love tailoring our time together to make sure they come home happy, tired, and ready for a nap.

Reliability and safety are my top priorities. I know that your dog is a member of your family, and I take that responsibility seriously. You can count on me to be punctual, attentive to your dog’s needs, and communicative with frequent updates and photos. I’m not just a dog walker; I’m a new best friend for your pup who will give them the love, exercise, and attention they deserve while you’re busy.
